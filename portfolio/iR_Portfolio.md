# ilo sama RAIL - Portfolio
Hiya, here's our (mostly Nagi's) portfolio of things we have for various simulators/games, but especially Train Sim World. Some things are now cancelled, some are still work in progress, but we're proud to how them off nonetheless!

## Train Sim World - Official

### DB BR 101 Expert - 101 066 "Einziganders" Decal
For TSG's/CRG's BR 101 Expert DLCs I have supplied a decal texture for the 101 066 "Einziganders" pride livery. My tasks included sourcing reference photos, stitching up those images, and touching up various spots (weathering, seams, etc). I remade the texture for this release after having already published an older (less polished) version as a free, unofficial modification.

## Train Sim World - Unofficial

### BR 423 Expansion Pack
For our own BR 423 Expansion Pack mod I was responsible for various systems and complex materials within Train Sim World. The highlights of the materials are the front LCD PIS done by Hannah under my instructions, and the side LCD PIS and EBuLa material done by yours truly.

![](img/423_ExP/iR_423_ExP_EBuLa.png)
*Screenshot of our EBuLa system within Train Sim World 6 showing various timetable information onboard a BR 423*

![](img/423_ExP/iR_423_ExP_SideLCD.png)
*Screenshot of our custom LCD PIS material on the side of a BR 423. Notably, each pixel consists of multiple subpixels influencing the shape of the letters*

![](img/423_ExP/iR_423_ExP_FrontLCD.png)
*Screenshot of our LCD PIS material at the front of a BR 423. This was Hannah's first PIS material venture, done under my instruction and supervision.*

The programming of our custom timetable information system supplying the PIS and EBuLa with data was written entirely by me from scratch; the EBuLa blueprints were a joint venture between me and Hannah.

![](img/423_ExP/iR_423_ExP_TDM_BP.png)

### Rankbachbahn
The *Rankbachbahn* is my current route project for Train Sim World. Model-wise, I have only focused on new rail network assets for now; mostly new station PIS screens and track assets.

**New station PIS screens**  
These screens are representing the most current software version running on the german rail network and as such are more complex compared to the displays we have in TSW nowadays. The material, fonts and symbols were painstakingly done by me, though the display itself is yet to be implemented into our timetable data system.
